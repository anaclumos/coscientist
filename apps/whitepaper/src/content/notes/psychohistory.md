# Psychohistory

Psychohistory is a fictional discipline in Isaac Asimov's Foundation series. It combines history, sociology, and mathematics to predict the large-scale behavior of civilizations.

It is the scientific basis for the [Seldon Plan](./seldon-plan) and the creation of the [Encyclopedia Galactica](./encyclopedia-galactica).
