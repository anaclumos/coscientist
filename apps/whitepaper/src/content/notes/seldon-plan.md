# Seldon Plan

The Seldon Plan is Hari Seldon's long-range plan to reduce the duration of a post-collapse dark age. It is derived from [Psychohistory](./psychohistory) and sets the context for the [Encyclopedia Galactica](./encyclopedia-galactica).
